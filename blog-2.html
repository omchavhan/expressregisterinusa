Can an indian Register a company in USA?

by: Stenve Martin   /   Jan 26, 2017

Yes, an Indian can register a company in the United States. The process is straightforward and not very difficult, but there are some things you’ll want to take into account before filing your paperwork.

Here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Familiarize yourself with the requirements of registering a company in the United States. There are specific laws and regulations that must be followed, and if you don’t understand them, an attorney may be able to help you out.

2. Make sure your business is legally structured and compliant with all applicable regulations. If your company includes individuals who are not US citizens or permanent residents, for example, make sure you have proper visas in place.

3. Prepare your paperwork correctly and file it on time. Incorrect filings can result in delays or even rejection of your application.

4. Keep records of all your dealings with US authorities, including copies of all contracts, meeting minutes, and correspondence from lawyers or other advisors. This information can be useful if any issues arise down the road.
