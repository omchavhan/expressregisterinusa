CHANGE OF REGISTERED AGENTS SERVICES

Why Change Registered Agents with ExpressRegister?

Changing the registered agent without notifying the Secretary of State may be penalized by loss of license, fines, and the right to enter into legal agreements and/or access to the state court system. The reinstatement process may also present additional financial and legal difficulties.


To change the Enrollment Agent, you must first complete and submit an Enrollment Agent Enrollment Change. This submission allows you to stop using your current Enrollment Agent service and choose a new Enrollment Agent. Leave the paperwork to us. Save time.

Privacy

The registered agent's address is known. It is also the address used to serve litigation and other legal documents. Do not receive service in front of friends or family.

Stable commercial offices

Our registered agents' offices are on-premises and manned during normal business hours, so you don't have to worry about losing important documents.

Compliance check-ups

We will let you know if we need to file papers with the state. In many cases, we work with our registered agents to make it as easy as one-click sign-up.
